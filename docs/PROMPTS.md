# LastroForge: Visual Generation Prompts

Use these prompts in Midjourney v6, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion to generate the visual assets for your artifacts.

## Aesthetic DNA
*High-end technical minimalism, dark luxury, cinematic lighting, floating translucent geometry, 8k resolution, octane render.*

---

### 1. The Genesis Coin (Standard Market)
> "A close-up of a futuristic digital coin floating in a void. The surface is made of polished liquid obsidian and etched with glowing neon amber circuits. The center is a hollow prism. Minimalist, premium tech aesthetic, sharp focus, 8k."

### 2. The Debt Relic (Financial Artifact)
> "An abstract 3D sculpture of a rotating crystalline gear. Inside the crystal, captured lightning pulses. The lighting is cold blue and sterile white. Shot with a macro lens. High-end fintech luxury, translucent, 8k."

### 3. Sovereign Meta-Identity (Identity Market)
> "A translucent 3D human skull mask made of frosted glass and interwoven with golden fiber-optic threads. Floating in a dark atmosphere. Ethereal, cyberpunk elegance, intricate detail, 8k."

### 4. Void-Backed Shard (High Scarcity)
> "A jagged fragment of a pitch-black asteroid floating in zero gravity. The fragment is leaking pure white light from its cracks. Cinematic, cosmic horror minimalism, high contrast, 8k."

### 5. Liquid Utility Node (Gaming/Metaverse)
> "A floating sphere of iridescent mercury-like liquid that defies gravity. Around it, microscopic drones form a geometric ring. Vibrant teal and magenta highlights. Playful but sophisticated, 8k."

### 6. The Eternal Pact (Luxury Asset)
> "Two interlocking rings made of white marble and brass, hovering over a velvet dark surface. Gold dust particles suspended in the air. Time-frozen, editorial photography feel, 8k."
